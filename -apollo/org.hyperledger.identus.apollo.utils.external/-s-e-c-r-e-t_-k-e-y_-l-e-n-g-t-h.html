SECRET_KEY_LENGTH

external var SECRET_KEY_LENGTH: Any(source)

Represents the length of a secret key.

The SECRET_KEY_LENGTH variable is used to specify the length of a secret key. It is of type Any and should be assigned with the required length of the secret key.

Note that the length should be an integer value representing the number of characters or bits in the key.
